Game Rules

This is a simplified version of 5 card draw poker. To start the player must ante $25 to draw 5 cards. If the player does not have enough money they can not ante and must refreshthe page.

Once the player has drawn 5 cards they can click on ones that they would like to discard. Selected cards to discard will be flipped over and can be unflipped if the player decides to keep it instead. Once the player is happy with the cards to discard select discard, this costs an additional $25.

Game notes:

  • This game considers ace to be only high, ie. ace can not be played in A, 2, 3, 4, 5.
  • Kicker cards are not considered in determining a winner. If player and computer have the same main hand it will be considered a tie.

Poker Hands

  1. Royal Flush: Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10 of the same suit
  2. Straight Flush: Five cards in a row of the same suit
  3. Four of a Kind: Four of the same card
  4. Full House: A pair and three of a kind
  5. Flush: Five cards of the same suit
  6. Straight: Five cards in a row any suit
  7. Three of a Kind: Three of the same card
  8. Two Pair: Two pairs of cards
  9. Pair: Two of the same card
  10. High Card: If no other hand, highest value card in hand
